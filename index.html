Timeline

A live and interative timeline for recording important events, expectations, and promises made between the Congressional Committees on Veterans Affairs and the Department of Veterans Affairs.

  • December 15th 2022

    GAO Report Release

    On 12/15/22, The Government Accountability Office (GAO) released a report, titled: “VA IT Contracting: Observations on Trends, Competition, and Past Performance Information”

    The report found that the VA's contract obligations increased as the number of contracts awarded decreased, meaning, the VA was not fostering a competitive and rigorous environment for contracting as commensurate to the resources provided to them by congress and the American people. This report prompted acting Committee Chair Mark Takano to formally request that several documents be issued to congress by February first. The GAO article: https://www.gao.gov/products/gao-23-105446

  • Early 2023

    Supply Chain RFP

    Congress expects the VA Supply Chain RFP in early 2023. Micheal Parrish testified to House Committee on Veterans Affairs on 9/22 at the hearing titled: "Major Acquisition Failures" that the Supply Chain RFP will be released in "early 2023" although he did not provide any specific timeframe. During the hearing Ranking Member Tracy Mann asked the question "When will you issue the Supply Chain RFP that is late?". During the same hearing Parrish made the appeal and promise several times that the late Acquisition Life Cycle Framework (ALF)'s release would coincide with the release of this expected Supply Chain RFP.

  • Early 2023

    Document Requested: Committee Member Jon Tester

    Committee Member Jon Tester's office issued a formal request for a copy of the final Institute for Defense Analyses independent life cycle cost estimate of the Electronic Health Record Modernization program (EHRM) and any related presentations or other relevant documents.

    A due date was not specified in the official letter requesting these documents. However, Mark Takano has at the same time requested the exact same document with a due date of February 1st 2023.

  • February 1st 2023

    Document Requested: Committee Member Mark Takano (Due 2/1/23)

    Committee Chair Mark Takano's office issued a formal request for a copy of the final Institute for Defense Analyses (IDA) independent life cycle cost estimate (LCCE) of the Electronic Health Record Modernization (EHRM) program.

  • February 1st 2023

    Document Requested: Committee Member Mark Takano (Due 1/2/23)

    Committee Chair Mark Takano's office issued a formal request for a list of all currently active contract awards on the T4NG contract vehicle to include: Awardee, Award Date, End Date, Contracting Ceiling Amount, Amount Obligated to Date, and Overview of service or product provided.
